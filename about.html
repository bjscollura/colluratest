Thanks for taking the time! I’m Brian - I’m a User Experience Designer, a Web Developer, and an IT Engineer. Over the last 10 years, I’ve seen Full Stack development replace traditional web teams, I’ve watched BDD and DevOps automation commoditize the workflow, and I’ve helped implement frameworks and SaaS suites for my Applications teams as they came in and out of vogue. The pace of change has been rapid.

But I’m also a Social Psychologist and a Customer Service Professional. While these burgeoning web technologies were made by well-trained and well-intentioned technologists, the tools and the workflows that emerge around them don’t allow coders to anticipate customer confusions and needs, testing user BEHAVIORS instead of user INTENTIONS, and they’ve replaced the number of humans who used to discover these big-picture issues, in exchange for context-unaware tools and AI.

My skillset, my passion, and my primary aggravation are all the same - bring customer service back to automated experiences. Someday, when we the consumers have the same control over our tech as we do our hammers and nails, I will retire, and be content with the world I’ve helped leave for the rest of us.
